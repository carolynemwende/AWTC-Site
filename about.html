ABOUT AWTC

Women continue to be excluded from conversations on policy formulation, decision-mak-ing and equity in the technology sector. AkiraChix works to facilitate an environment for women in tech and those aspiring to connect, network and find solutions to the challenges they face in the industry. We believe women can be the drivers, the creators, the consum-ers, the decision makers and leaders in problem solving of current and emerging issues and trends in the information and communication technology field.The Africa Women in Tech Conference (AWTC) by AkiraChix is the largest gathering of wom-en in tech in Africa whose main objectives are:

  • Create opportunities and tools that allow women to grow, learn and develop across the tech industry.
  • Expose women to emerging technologies to increase capacity and knowledge.
  • Provide a safe space for networking leading to increased mentorship as well as strengthen our collective and diverse voices.
  • Showcase women innovators and honor contributions made by women in the tech community.

Building a Better Community through education, networking
and credible, industry news.
